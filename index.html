Kootsinta

Software used: Adobe Xd (UI Layout), Adobe Illustrator (Logo)

A food ordering and delivery application centered for Filipino sweet delicacies. KootSinta is inspired by a Filipino rice cake called kutsinta. To add playfulness into it, the letter u was replaced by a double o that turned into 2 kutsinta shaped like a heart. The name is also mixed with the word sinta, meaning "lover", because who wouldn't fall in love with something sweet?
